TEAM

Project Manager: Responsible for overall planning, execution, and monitoring of the project. They coordinate resources, manage stakeholders, and ensure the project meets its objectives.

Team Lead:Leads a specific subset of the project team, often focusing on a particular deliverable or aspect of the project. They report to the project manager and coordinate activities within their team.

Developers/Engineers: Responsible for implementing technical solutions, coding, testing, and deploying software or hardware components of the project.

Designers: Create visual and user experience designs, ensuring that the project meets aesthetic and usability standards.

Analysts: Gather and analyze data, providing insights that inform decision-making throughout the project lifecycle.
